What makes Argon different? +
Argon uses advanced entropy modeling and adaptive quantization to deliver the highest compression ratios while preserving 100% visual fidelity. It's both faster and smaller than traditional codecs like PNG or WebP.

Does it work offline? +
Absolutely. Argon runs completely offline with no internet dependency. Your images never leave your system, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Which file formats are supported? +
Argon supports PNG, JPEG, TIFF, BMP, and RAW formats for input, and outputs highly optimized .argon or .jxl containers for best performance.
